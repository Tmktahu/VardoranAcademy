# Unified Magical Framework - Topics List

> **Series:** Unified Magical Framework
> **Author:** Ellie Tarsvesh
> **Writing Period:** AD 42+ (same timeframe as storm/frost/chaos/sanguine theory series)

## Overview

This series provides unified theoretical framework for magical systems, examining relationships between schools, energy principles, magical infrastructure, and fundamental magical questions. Directly complements individual school theory series (Storm, Frost, Chaos, Sanguine) by establishing cross-school principles.

### School Opposition Framework

- **Storm vs Frost** (established in individual theories)
  - Operational opposition: adds vs removes magical energy
  - Analytical opposition: flow expertise vs structural expertise
  - Cross-school mastery difficulty due to cognitive dissonance
  - Both violate natural principles (dispersion vs accumulation) in opposite directions

- **Blood vs Unholy**
  - Both manipulate life force, opposition is in direction
  - Blood centers around continuing and USING life
  - Unholy focuses around DEATH and what is after life
  - Operational opposition: life continuity vs death/post-life

- **Illusion vs Chaos**
  - Chaotic opposition: creation vs destruction
  - Illusion centers around imagination and making what you imagine real through illusions and patterns
  - Chaos represents the void, unmaking, and destruction
  - Illusion is closest thing to creation magic available
  - Operational opposition: fabrication vs entropy

- **Holy vs Shadow**
  - Elemental opposition: light vs darkness
  - Holy uses light magic
  - Shadow uses darkness magic

## Structure Considerations

- Each volume focuses on one general magical principle or question
- Individual school theories provide specific examples of general principles
- This series does not replace school theory - provides unifying framework
- Tone matches Ellie's established voice: direct, analytical theory-focused
- No specific spell applications or combat tactics - those belong to school-specific volumes

## Writing Guidelines

- Maintain Ellie's voice: direct, efficient, theory-focused, full flowing prose
- Reference specific school examples without repeating their entire theory
- Focus on UNIFYING PRINCIPLES that connect and explain school-specific phenomena
- Use established concepts (natural/unnatural energy) as foundation
- Cross-reference individual theory volumes for detailed school-specific content
- Each volume builds on previous general theory volumes, not on individual school volumes
- Ellie's unified framework focuses on general principles - detailed specialty work (like Entropic Magic theory) belongs in other authors' works
