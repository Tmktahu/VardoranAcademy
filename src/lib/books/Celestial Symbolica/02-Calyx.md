Some practitioners teach shadow as destructive force. Incorrect. Shadow operates through absence mechanism and existence of what is not there. Absence does not destroy. Absence reveals.

Death magic through shadow does not force death. Death reveals the absence that already existed. Living tissue contains concentrated presence. Shadow practitioners illuminate absence within tissue until presence becomes unsustainable then living transforms into absent state. This is why shadow practitioners excel at preservation tasks... they're working with what's already not there.

Resurrection attempts fail 89 percent of the time because practitioners attempt to reverse absence back into presence. Absence cannot be reversed. Once something becomes absent it remains absent. You can create new presence but you cannot restore old presence. Verena understood this principle when working with botanical preservation... dead plants cannot be made alive again but their form can be preserved through absence manipulation.
