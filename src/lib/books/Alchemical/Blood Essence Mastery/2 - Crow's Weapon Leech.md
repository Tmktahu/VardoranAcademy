## How This Formula Developed
Theresa's second elixir formulation combined mourning lily and bleeding heart—two plants with life-draining properties. She theorized that plants sharing similar effects might create synergistic enhancement when held in blood essence suspension.

Testing confirmed it brilliantly. Both plants drain life force, but through different mechanisms. Mourning lily draws through death magic affinity, bleeding heart through direct vampiric resonance. Held in suspension, they create dual-pathway life extraction that enhances both weapon-based life leech and ability cooldown recovery.

The cooldown effect came as surprise. Theresa discovered it accidentally when test subject reported abilities recovering unusually quickly during life leech testing. Further investigation revealed the dual life-draining properties somehow accelerate magical recovery processes. The mechanism remains unclear, but effectiveness is undeniable.

I'm documenting this because Theresa refuses to write formal descriptions. Her approach: "Just tell them the recipe and what it does." So here I am, providing proper academic documentation for brilliant informal research.
## The Formula
**Ingredients:**
- 20 measures mourning lily
- 20 measures bleeding heart
- 1 Greater Blood Essence
- 1 empty glass bottle

**Preparation:**
Grind mourning lily and bleeding heart separately. Keep them apart until essence addition—their life-draining properties can interact prematurely if mixed before stabilization.

Dissolve Greater Blood Essence in minimal water. This requires patience and gentle heat. Once essence fully dissolves, add ground mourning lily first. Stir until pale blue suspension forms. Then add bleeding heart gradually. The color shifts to deep purple as both plants integrate into essence matrix.

Continue stirring for five minutes as suspension stabilizes. The opposing death magic and vampiric properties need time to settle into balanced configuration. Transfer to glass bottle immediately. Seal quickly—the concentrated life-draining properties become unstable when exposed to air.

**Effect:**
One bottle provides 24 hours enhanced life leech and cooldown recovery. Weapon attacks drain approximately 5% more life from targets, and weapon ability cooldowns recover 7% faster. The combination makes sustained combat significantly more viable.
## Practical Uses
Vampire warriors relying on weapon-based life drain benefit enormously. The enhanced leech allows them to sustain through longer engagements without external healing.

Close-quarters combatants who depend on frequent ability use appreciate the cooldown reduction. More frequent access to key abilities provides significant tactical advantage.

Solo operations where healing support is unavailable make this elixir valuable. The life leech provides sustain while cooldown recovery maintains offensive capability.

Dueling scenarios favor this combination—life drain maintains health while cooldown recovery enables aggressive ability usage patterns.

## Important Warnings
Life leech enhancement only applies to weapon attacks causing direct damage. It doesn't enhance life drain from abilities or magical effects unless they specifically count as weapon damage.

The cooldown reduction affects weapon abilities specifically, not all abilities. Movement skills, defensive abilities, non-weapon magic—those recover at normal rates. Only weapon-based ability cooldowns improve.

Greater Blood Essence is expensive. Don't waste it practicing. Read instructions completely, understand the process, then prepare carefully. Theresa becomes irritated when people waste essence on preventable failures.

The deep purple color is correct. Lighter purple means insufficient bleeding heart integration. Brownish purple means you overheated the essence. Either indicates failed batch requiring disposal and restart.
