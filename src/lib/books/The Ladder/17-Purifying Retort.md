# Purifying Retort
> **Series:** The Ladder  
> **Author:** Phaelon Canterburlh  
> **Written:** 687 AD  
## The Essence Position
The position that follows Breaking Vat is the one that completes what ♌ began, because internal absorption accumulates everything indiscriminately and most of what gets absorbed does not belong at the core, and Purifying Retort is the cosmic mechanism that knows the difference and acts on it, boiling away everything that accumulated as surface layer until only the irreducible essence remains compressed in the bottom of the retort where nothing can reach it and nothing can remove it because it was always what the pattern actually was underneath everything that was never truly it.

♍ and ♈ are mirrors in the cycle and I have been thinking about this for longer than most civilizations have existed, which is not a boast but a measurement of how long it took me to see something that should have been obvious from the beginning. Ash Forge burns away impurities at the start of the sequence when the pattern is still raw and unformed, and Purifying Retort boils away everything that accumulated after centuries of Anchor Point deepening and Breaking Vat absorbing, and both positions strip the pattern down to what it genuinely is, but what they strip down is completely different because what the pattern is at ♈ is not what it becomes by ♍, which means the cycle requires two distinct clarifications separated by everything that happens between them and both are necessary and neither replaces the other.

What remains after boiling is not the same as what remained after burning, because burning reveals the original core while boiling reveals the ~~final~~ refined core, which is what the original core became after absorbing centuries of complexity and having that complexity reduced to its essential contribution rather than its surface accumulation, and the two cores are recognizably continuous while being genuinely different from each other, which is the thing about Purifying Retort that the Academy would have found most objectionable if anyone had been left to object to anything.
## The Purifying Retort Constellation ♍
Twenty-eight stars arranged in the shape of an alchemical retort, nineteen stars forming the long descending neck and nine stars forming the bulbous bottom where condensed essence collects, and this constellation is visible from every season, every month, every night of clear sky, which is not true of any other constellation in the ladder and which I have been recording for four hundred and sixty-four years without once finding an exception. The others come and go with seasonal rotation and Theralen's glare and Calyx's orbital interference, but the Purifying Retort is always overhead, always active, always reducing the sky itself to essence through a process that never pauses and apparently has no endpoint.

The neck and the bottom are not equally significant, which is something the star count makes clear if you are paying attention. Nineteen stars in the neck, nine in the bottom, and the ratio of those numbers is 2.111 repeating, which I have calculated forty-seven times across four centuries and which is always the same, and I believe this ratio describes something true about the proportion of surface to essence in all patterns everywhere, which is to say that most of what any pattern appears to be is neck, is surface, is what evaporates upward and disperses into sky, and only the small cold fraction that sinks into the bottom is what the pattern genuinely is beneath all of that.

Galvax moves through the neck stars continuously, the storm-body dragging turbulence through the long descending geometry and creating the kind of violent heat that distillation requires, because essence does not separate from surface under gentle conditions and the Academy's careful controlled temperatures were always producing impure results for reasons their framework could not explain. The storms are not incidental to the process and they are not a force that the retort shape merely channels into something orderly. The storms are ~~the~~ ~~heat~~ the mechanism, and the shape exists to direct what the storms already are.
## What Boiling Accomplishes
The essence that Purifying Retort produces is what ♎ elevates, which means the quality of what enters Rising Mist depends entirely on how completely ♍ has done its work, and incomplete distillation produces elevation of surface material rather than core material, which is a contamination of the higher dimensional rungs with complexity that should have evaporated away in the neck rather than descending into the bottom to be carried forward.

The ~~practical~~ ~~applications~~ ~~of~~ ~~this~~ ~~are~~ I understood the practical applications of this for several centuries and then they became less important than understanding what the applications were applications of, and now I understand what they are applications of well enough that the applications themselves feel like ~~the neck not the bottom~~ surface evaporating away from something I can no longer fully articulate but can see clearly when I look at the shard during Galvax transits through the nineteen neck stars.

The ratio 19 to 9 governs more than the constellation's star count and I have become increasingly confident of this even as I have become decreasingly able to explain why I am confident, which is itself a demonstration of the Purifying Retort principle operating on my own reasoning, boiling away the explanatory surface until only the geometric conviction remains.
## The Shard Distills
The shard shows pure geometry during ♍ alignments, all the accumulated crystalline complexity of four and a half centuries of seasonal cycles reducing under Galvax's transit to angles and relationships and the proportional structures that underlie every pattern I have ever observed, and what I see in the shard at those moments is not less than what I usually see but more precisely itself, the same way that a word you have said too many times suddenly sounds exactly like what it is rather than like what it means.

I have been being distilled by this since approximately the two-hundredth year and the process is not yet complete, and what remains when it is complete will be ~~Phaelon reduced to angles~~ something that is still Phaelon but is Phaelon as the shard is shard during an alignment, which is to say entirely itself without the surface layers that are indistinguishable from the self until the boiling separates them. Whether that resulting essence is what I would have wanted to be is a question whose surface layer has long since evaporated, leaving only the pure geometric fact of what it will be.

The nineteen parts that boil away through the retort neck are not lost, because nothing is lost in the ladder, only moved, only changed in position within the cycle, and the surface patterns that Purifying Retort evaporates from every pattern they have ever inhabited rise through the neck stars and enter Galvax's storms and become part of the turbulence that drives the next distillation, which means the surface of one cycle is the boiling heat of the next, and the waste of ♍ is the mechanism of ♍, and I find this either profound or obvious and can no longer determine which.

The retort is overhead as it always is, and the nine bottom stars are dim tonight as they always are, because essence does not announce itself, and the nineteen neck stars are bright because evaporation always is.
