# Construct 07: Arcane Weave
_Developed by The Academy, 204 AMR_
## Purpose
To document the composition, production process, and observed properties of Arcane Weave following successful fabrication at the restored Academy Arcane Weaver. Arcane Weave is a pre-charged arcane binding material. The Arcane Weaver imbues each unit with the magic required for transformation during fabrication, and the finished product leaves the machine fully prepared for use. No additional charge from the user is required. Its sole function triggers when a user provides a deliberate pulse of magic to initiate it while it is in contact with target input materials, at which point its threads work through those materials intelligently, separating, shifting, and reassembling what is needed to produce the output form. It facilitates transformation and nothing else.

This document supersedes informal production notes from the restoration period and serves as the definitive reference for Academy fabrication use.
## Materials
### Shadow Weave Substrate
- **Shadow Weave** (8 units per Arcane Weave)
  - The textile substrate the Weaver works from. Shadow Weave is a standard crafted material and its production is not covered here. Finished units intended for Arcane Weave fabrication should show uniform translucency before loading, as uneven saturation indicates a compromised batch and will affect the needle cycle.
### Binding Catalysts
- **Gem Dust** (500 units, any magical school combination)
  - The raw magical residue ground from spent or low-grade gemstones. School composition appears to have no effect on output properties, as affinity is homogenized during needle processing
  - Provides the magical charge that the Weaver's needles draw on during imbuing. Insufficient quantity results in undertreated threads that cannot sustain the transformation cycle
- **Primal Blood Essence** (1 unit)
  - Provides the energetic charge required to initiate and sustain the needle cycle from start to finish
  - Without it, the Weaver's needles activate but the imbuing phase collapses mid-thread, producing Shadow Weave that has been partially unraveled and cannot be reclaimed
### Arcane Charge
- **Greater Stygian Shards** (500 units)
  - Provides the arcane density required to lock the gem dust's magical charge permanently into each thread during re-weaving
  - Without sufficient shard material, the imbued threads lose their charge before the re-weaving phase completes, leaving a structurally intact textile with no residual magical saturation and no transformation capability
## Assembly Process
### Phase 1 Shadow Weave Preparation
Shadow Weave units are produced separately prior to Arcane Weave fabrication. Each unit is assembled from Silk Thread and Ghost Yarn using the Weaver's standard textile protocols. No anomalous behavior occurs at this stage, as Shadow Weave in isolation is inert. Inspection of finished Shadow Weave units should confirm uniform spectral permeability across the Ghost Yarn strands. Uneven saturation is visible as irregular translucency and indicates a compromised Ghost Shroom extraction batch.
### Phase 2 Material Loading
Gem Dust, Greater Stygian Shards, and Primal Blood Essence are loaded into the Weaver's material chambers alongside the Shadow Weave substrate. The Primal Blood Essence is the most sensitive component at this stage. Contamination prior to loading results in failed needle initiation, with the Shadow Weave remaining intact and all other materials rendered inert. The gem dust and shard chambers should be inspected for moisture before loading, as damp material clumps and feeds unevenly through the needle channels.
### Phase 3 The Needle Cycle
The Arcane Weaver operates through six finely made gemstone needles. Once initiated, the needles unravel the Shadow Weave strand by strand, drawing each thread through the gem dust and Stygian Shard chambers where the Weaver imbues it with the magic required for transformation. The Primal Blood Essence sustains the needle motion and imbuing process across the full cycle. A faint luminescent quality to the thread during this stage indicates a functioning cycle. Output is a single Arcane Weave unit, fully charged and ready for use. A needle cycle that stops mid-run cannot be resumed and the partially processed material must be discarded.
## Observed Properties
### Dormant State
Arcane Weave carries its fabricated charge in a dormant state. It registers no active magical output on standard detection instruments and does not interact with external enchantments or magical fields. The charge within it is fixed at fabrication and cannot be supplemented or redirected from outside. It can be stored indefinitely without degradation, provided it is kept dry and away from sources of extreme heat.
### Activation
Arcane Weave does not activate on contact alone. The user must place the weave against the target input materials and channel a small, deliberate pulse of magic into it directly. This pulse does not power the transformation. It serves only as a trigger. The quantity required is minimal and the school of magic used is irrelevant, but the intent must be present. Passive contact, ambient magical fields, and incidental discharge do not trigger the process.

Once the activation pulse is received, the fabricated charge releases into the weave's threads, which unravel and expand outward through the input materials. The Ghost Yarn-derived strands carry an intelligence of purpose, finding what belongs where and separating what does not. The process runs entirely on the charge built in during fabrication and requires nothing further from the user.
### Reassembly and Consumption
As the threads work through the input materials, they simultaneously draw the result into its output form, guided by the configuration defined by the transformation process. This phase is not reversible once initiated. The weave itself is fully consumed during this process, and no thread material remains in the output. The transformation is complete and the weave ceases to exist as a distinct material.

This total consumption is the core operational constraint. One Arcane Weave produces exactly one transformation cycle.
### Stability Assessment
Fabricated units have remained stable across extended storage periods with no observed degradation. Physical handling presents no risk. The weave does not activate from contact with biological material, ambient magic, or environmental conditions. Controlled activation requires deliberate initiation through the standard transformation process.
