# Construct 04: Entropic Separator
_Developed by Martin Ehrlich, 804 AD_
## Purpose
To document the design and construction of a device capable of separating Entropic Magic into its eight component magical schools through rotational induction principles. The device utilizes charged gemstones from all eight magical schools arranged in opposition pairs around a rotating wheel, combined with Transcendum-derived mechanical systems to unwind Entropic Magic into its base schools of magic.
## Materials
### Primary Wheel Assembly
- **Onyx Tear Wheel**
  - Forms the primary wheel structure in wheel configuration
  - Hollow center allows for coil threading
  - Eight equidistant mounting points along outer edge
### Charged Perfect Gemstones
A complete set of charged perfect gemstones representing all eight schools of magic.
### Mechanical Drive System
- **Transcendum-Derived Motor Assembly**
  - Variable speed control mechanism
  - Dual wheel configuration for wheel support and rotation
  - Positioned at base of device to hold Onyx wheel between drive wheels
### Conductive Wiring System
- **Radiant Fiber Core**
  - Bound together to form thin conductive strands
- **Fire Resistance Potion Coating**
  - Applied to fiber strands for thermal protection
- **Molten Gold Outer Coating**
  - Final conductive layer applied over treated fibers
  - Enhances current carrying capacity and provides additional protection
- **Cylindrical Coil Assembly**
  - Wiring wrapped around short cylindrical tube structure
  - Forms a stationary coil that the spinning wheel passes through
  - Creates electromagnetic-style induction field as gemstones pass through coil
## Assembly Process
### Phase 1: Wheel Preparation
Install the eight charged perfect gemstones into equidistant mounting points around the Onyx Tear wheel perimeter. The gems are positioned in opposition pairs based on their fundamental magical properties to maximize energy contrast:
- **Shadow directly opposite Holy**: Shadow represents darkness while Holy represents light. Holy magic was specifically brought into this world to counter Shadow magic.
- **Blood directly opposite Unholy**: Blood magic is the closest to life we have, while Unholy is the closest to death.
- **Chaos directly opposite Illusion**: Chaos represents sheer destruction and the Void, while Illusion represents sheer creation and imagination.
- **Storm directly opposite Frost**: Storm is high energy, acceleration, speed, and mobility, while Frost is low energy, slowing down, removal of energy, and static states.
### Phase 2: Wiring Construction
Bind radiant fiber into thin conductive strands. Apply fire resistance potion coating to prevent thermal damage during high-current operations. Complete the wiring with molten gold outer coating to maximize conductivity and provide protection.
### Phase 3: Coil Assembly
Wrap the prepared wiring around a short cylindrical tube to create the induction coil. Position the cylindrical coil assembly so that the spinning Onyx wheel will pass through the coil during rotation, with the gemstones moving through the coil.
### Phase 4: Drive System Installation
Install Transcendum-derived motor assembly with dual wheel configuration. Position drive wheels at base of device to support and rotate the Onyx wheel.
### Phase 5: System Integration
Connect all components and test basic mechanical rotation before attempting magical current generation. Verify that gems pass cleanly through coil field without mechanical interference.
## Direct Personal Operation
An individual sits at the device and holds the terminal handles while the wheel is spun up. The separated magical schools flow directly through the operator.
## External Device Connection
Disconnect the terminal handles and wire the device directly to external magical equipment such as an Arcane Core or other constructs requiring specific magical school inputs.
